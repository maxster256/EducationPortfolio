Fall 2017

I look back fondly to the third semester here at WUST. I've certainly learned a lot of important skills related to the more advanced programming in Java, Gang of Four's Design Patterns and web frameworks.

I've also gained a lot of knowledge regarding the IT architecture, a subject which was quite hard, frankly, but absolutely worth the while. And the probability-related topics were cool, too, especially since they might come in handy in the future.

Technologie Programowania

This course has allowed me to focus on various technologies related to more "professional" programming. I've learned a lot about design patterns, team work, Java frameworks and testing.

What I've learned
  • Play to nie jest framework dla leniwych ludzi

  • Webówkę chyba lepiej jednak klepać w Pythonie

  • Jak wysłać Pana Rafała do macierzy

AKiSO

Certainly the most demanding course, but at the same time extremely rewarding. Learned a lot about low-level programming, computer systems, their history and architecture. Everything was pretty much top-notch on this course.

What I've learned
  • To jest naprawdę bardzo proste
  • Prowadzący nie wymaga, żebyśmy pisali programów w Assemblerze, tylko potrafili je czytać
  • When in doubt, trust tabelka Carnaugh
  • Ręczna odbudowa tablicy alokacji w systemie plików FAT jest naprawdę bardzo prosta
  • Dr Kik czasem brzmi jak Dr Zawada przez telefon
What I've yet to learn
  • Jak napisać dodawanie, mając do dyspozycji tylko XOR-y i NAND-e
  • Jak oddać zadanie z systemu plików FAT
