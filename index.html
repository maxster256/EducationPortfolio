About the site

The site was written in pure HTML 5 and CSS 3 with the mobile-first approach. In terms of the rules applied here:

  • Contrast is applied as much as possible, though the site is mostly kept in minimal fashion.
  • Repetitions are pretty much omnipresent here, but without too much excess.
  • Aligment of the elements is carefully planned, with the Golden Rule and the common sense especially kept in mind.
  • Proximity of the objects which belong to the same group is kept.

The negative space is used to make some room for breathing, and the colors are used to draw the user's attention to the UI features.

No frameworks or other technologies were used here apart from the beforementioned.
